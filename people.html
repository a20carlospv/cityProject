People

As of December 31, 2021, Sanxenxo had 17,856 registered in its municipal register.
This figure, which could undergo some small adjustments, is the highest population figure in its historical population series and represents a growth of 108% in the last century.
And it is that in 1920 Sanxenxo had 8,578 inhabitants, a figure that fluctuated over the years, especially significant between 2014 and 2019.
